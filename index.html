What is Enclosed Auto Transport?

Enclosed auto transport involves shipping your vehicle in a fully enclosed trailer, shielding it from weather, road debris, and other external risks. Unlike open transport, which exposes vehicles to the elements, enclosed transport is tailored for luxury, classic, or exotic cars that require maximum protection.

AmeriFreight’s enclosed carriers use hydraulic lifts for safe loading and unloading, especially for low-clearance vehicles. This premium service is ideal for long-distance transport or harsh weather conditions, ensuring your vehicle arrives in showroom condition.

Key benefits of enclosed auto transport include:

  • Full Protection: Shields vehicles from rain, snow, dust, and debris, preserving their condition.
  • Enhanced Security: Enclosed trailers conceal vehicles, reducing theft or vandalism risks.
  • Specialized Handling: Hydraulic lifts and smaller carrier capacities (1-4 vehicles) ensure careful transport.
  • Faster Delivery: Fewer stops mean quicker transit compared to open carriers.

Enclosed transport is the preferred choice for high-value vehicles, making it ideal for collectors, dealerships, and enthusiasts.
